Reception

Receptions at the Temple Inn & Suites

All Reception Packages The Reception rental from 4:00 pm-10:00 pm with and option to add past 10:00 pm at $250/hr up to Midnight. You and your guests are welcome to enjoy the Lobby, Grand Ballroom, Dining Room, Bride’s & Groom’s suites and Gardens. You are the only party on-site – you will not need to share space with other brides or compete for rooms and parking.

The Reception place provides large and spacious accommodations.

Please see our menus below for details about food policies and requirements

Discount pricing is available for daytime receptions from 10:00 am to 3:00 pm.

Receptions are fully customizable.
